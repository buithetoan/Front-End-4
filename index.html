Lightbridge is a set of tools for developers that allow for an effective,
stable and secure transmission of data from IoT devices.

Your data are secure. Always.

Data security is our top priority. We apply the highest standards to ensure that no unauthorized person is able to access data from your devices.

On devices themselves, data are secured based on state-of-the-art private key encryption methods. Only authorized users can access the keys.

IoT devices can generate huge amounts of data. Lightbridge uses proprietary 3X lossless compression algorithms coupled with optimized streaming technology to reduce storage capacity and transmission costs by 70%.

Multiple compression reduces transmission and energy costs, and thus battery lifetime. And all this without losing even a single byte of information!
